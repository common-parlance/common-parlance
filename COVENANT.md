This dataset is licensed under [ODC-BY 1.0](https://opendatacommons.org/licenses/by/1.0/) (Open Data Commons Attribution License). You are legally free to use it for any purpose, as long as you provide attribution.

This covenant is not a license. It is a statement of community expectations from the volunteers who contributed their conversations.

---

## The Ask

If you train a model on this dataset, we ask that you **publish your model weights openly**.

That's it.

No restrictions on architecture, fine-tuning approach, commercial use, or deployment. Just: if volunteer conversations helped build your model, let the community benefit from the result.

## Why

Every conversation in this dataset was donated by someone who chose to contribute to the open AI commons. They scrubbed their personal information, reviewed their conversations, and opted in. Not because they had to, but because they believe open models make AI safer, more accountable, and more useful for everyone.

Using this data to train a closed model takes from that commons without giving back.

## What We Consider Compliance

- Publishing model weights under any open license (Apache 2.0, MIT, CC-BY, etc.)
- Releasing on any public platform (HuggingFace, GitHub, etc.)
- No requirement to release training code, data mixtures, or infrastructure details

We are not asking you to open-source your entire stack. Just the weights.

## What If You Don't

This covenant isn't enforceable and we aren't interested in policing anyone. The license stands on its own.

We just hope that if this dataset helped your work, you'll consider paying it forward. Open weights make AI better for everyone.
