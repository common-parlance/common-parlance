# Contributor Terms

**Common Parlance**
Last updated: 2026-03-12

By granting consent to contribute conversations through Common Parlance, you
agree to the following terms:

## 1. Your Contributions

You represent that:

- The conversations you contribute are your own interactions with AI models
  running on your own hardware.
- You have the right to share these conversations and are not violating any
  agreements by doing so.
- You will not intentionally include content that infringes on the rights of
  others.

## 2. What Happens to Your Contributions

- Conversations are processed locally on your device to remove personally
  identifiable information (PII) before upload.
- Processed conversations are uploaded to a public dataset on HuggingFace.
- For 90 days after upload, contributions can be traced to your API key for
  deletion purposes. After 90 days, the mapping expires and contributions
  become permanently anonymous.
- Contributions are released under the dataset's license (see below) and may
  be used by anyone under those terms.

## 3. Dataset License

Contributions become part of the Common Parlance dataset, released under
[ODC-BY 1.0](https://opendatacommons.org/licenses/by/1.0/) (Open Data Commons
Attribution). This license allows anyone to use the data freely for any purpose with
attribution.

A [Community Covenant](COVENANT.md) asks (but does not legally require) that
models trained on this data release their weights openly.

By contributing, you agree to release your contributions under this license.
See LICENSE for the full text.

## 4. Content Standards

You agree not to intentionally submit:

- Illegal content of any kind
- Content that sexually exploits or endangers minors
- Content designed to facilitate violence or the creation of weapons
- Content you know contains the personal information of others

We reserve the right to remove any contribution without notice.

## 5. No Guarantees

Common Parlance is provided as-is. We make no guarantees about the
availability, accuracy, or completeness of the PII scrubbing process. You
should review your contributions before approving them for upload.

## 6. Changes

We may update these terms as the project evolves. Continued contributions
after changes constitute acceptance of the updated terms.
